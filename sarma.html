Sarma

Description

Sarma is the traditional Serbian dish that is widely popular in that country. It's moslty eaten in winter periods and in times of festive occasions.

It's and cabbage rolls filled with ground meat (usually pork and beef), it has lots of seasonings, chopped onion and rice.

Cabbage leaves that are used need to be fermented, and meat mixture needs to be carefuly wrapped in cabbage.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cabbage
  • 500g of ground pork meat
  • 100g of ground beef meat
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 cups of rice
  • 1 tablespoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 tablespoon of vegeta
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • few tablespoons of pork fat

Steps

  1. Prepare leaves by carefuly separating the leaves and cutting thick veins and rinsing them in water
  2. Mix beef and pork ground meet with your seasonings, chopped onions and minced garlick
  3. Saut√© minced meat just until it loses its red color
  4. To cooked meat add uncood rice and other spices and let it cook a little bit more so that it all gets together
  5. Wrap meat mixture in cabbage leaves and place them in a pot, in between the layers add a little fat
  6. Cover the sarma with water and let it cook for about 2 hours
  7. From time to time add some more water since rice drinks up a lot of it!
  8. When sarma is done, let it cool down a bit before serving and enjoj!
