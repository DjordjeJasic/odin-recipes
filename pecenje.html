Pecenje

Description

Pecenje is whole pig roasted on fire. It's special dish that is made for every big celebration in Serbia. Every big traditional resaurant has it, but not every has good pecenje

Pig is cooked on the open flames, not to strong fire for a few hours so that it stays juicy and it's skin gets crispy

Ingredients

  • 1 whole pig
  • 200gr of salt
  • pork fat
  • buthcers twine for sewing pig up
  • automatic spit
  • coals and some twigs
  • 2 beers

Steps

  1. Prepare pig, make little cuts on thicker parts of pig and pour salt in them, after you put it in cuts, take more salt and rub it all around it
  2. Then you need to take a little bit of pork fat and rub it over the pig, all around so it gets even more juicy
  3. Sal the insides of pig and add hefty amount of fat in there, after you do that you need to saw it up
  4. Use butchers twine and do the zig zag pattern
  5. After you do that you need to put pig on the spit and start roasting it
  6. When you start the fire you need to use all the twigs you have so you have good ground for fire
  7. Concetrate fire around thick parts of pig, like shoulders and thighs, not the stomach
  8. For 13kg pig you need to roast it for about 3 and a half hours
  9. When it's done you take one beer and shake it hard so that when you open it, it all explodes on the pig
  10. Other beer is for you for all hard work you do
  11. After that you need to remove it from fire and let it sit for a little bit and then dig right in
  12. Enjoy!
