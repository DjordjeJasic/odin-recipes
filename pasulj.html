Pasulj

Description

Pasulj is a traditional Serbian bean stew that is both hearty and flavorful. This dish is a beloved comfort food in Serbia, often enjoyed during colder months.

Pasulj can be made with or without meat, making it versatile for different dietary preferences. But, almost always it's with meat since that way is the best way.

Ingredients

  • 450g of beans (Serbian tetovac)
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 onions
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 garlic
  • 300g of dried meat
  • 1 tb of paprika
  • 1/2 tb of peper
  • 2 tbs of flour
  • 30 ml of oil
  • salt to the taste

Steps

  1. Clean the beans, and let them sit in water for a whole night
  2. Drain them from that water and let them cook until they boil, after that leave them cook for another 10 minutes
  3. Chop the onions and add them to the beans, after that add 2 bay leaves
  4. Chop carrots and saut√© them with onions and garlick
  5. To onions and carrots add meat and let it cook until it gets nice collor, it shouldn take long since everything will cook with beans
  6. When meat has nice color add everything to beans and let it cook for around 2 hours, add more water if it gets too thick
  7. When its done you need to make a roux
  8. For roux you need to heat oil and add flour, let it cook for a minute or two and then add paprika, mix it all together and add it to the beans
  9. Add salt and peper to the taste and let it cook for around 5 to 10 minutes
  10. When its done let it cool down a bit and enjoy!
